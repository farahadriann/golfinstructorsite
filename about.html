Meet Coach Terry Maratos

Terry Maratos is a PGA-certified golf instructor at Westchester Golf Course in Los Angeles.
With 5+ years of teaching experience, Terry brings fun, encouragement, and easy-to-follow tips for golfers of all skill levels.
“Golf is for everyone. My job is to make it welcoming, stress-free, and genuinely fun—so you want to come back again and again!”

  • ✔ Certified at Westchester Golf Course
  • ✔ Specializes in beginners & improvers
